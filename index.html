About Us

At Caffeno, we pride ourselves on being more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee or a bite to eat. We are a culinary destination that embraces the artistry of flavors, the joy of connection, and the spirit of community. Our journey began with a passion for creating a space where people can gather, unwind, and indulge in the simple pleasures of life. Every detail, from the carefully curated menu to the cozy and inviting atmosphere, has been thoughtfully designed to offer a memorable experience for our valued guests.

Our Menu

Latte

Our signature espresso, blended with plenty of silky smooth milk and finished.

Cappuccino

Espresso with steamed milk, microfoam, and dusted with chocolate powder.

Espresso

Nothing more than a single espresso shot of our classico blend.

Iced Latte

A simple mix of espresso coffee, milk and a hint of sweetness over ice.

Frappe

Our best selling coffee milkshake, made with our espresso, milk and ice.

Caramello

Two shots of our rich espresso, with steamed milk and a shot of caramel syrup.
